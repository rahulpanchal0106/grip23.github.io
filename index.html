About

Introducing an online basic banking system that helps making payments easy. Now you can transfer money from anywhere really easy. Just simply tap on the person you want to send funds, and enter their email and amount to be transfered. Tap on the Send button and its done!

This was developed solely for an internship project. There is no actual money transfer happening. Names and other details mentioned in the customers' table is completely random. None of the persons listed in that table represents an existing real person.
